## Deviations from Plan

### Auto-fixed Issues

**1. [Rule 1 — Bug] riverpod_generator version mismatch with riverpod_annotation**
- **Found during:** Task 2 (`fvm flutter pub get` resolution)
- **Issue:** Plan specified `riverpod_generator: ^3.0.0` paired with `riverpod_annotation: ^4.0.2`. Pub solver rejected: `riverpod_generator >=3.0.0 <4.0.0+1 requires riverpod_annotation 3.0.0 or 3.0.1 or 3.0.2 or 3.0.3` — generator 3.x line is hard-pinned to annotation 3.x. Annotation 4.x requires generator 4.x. RESEARCH §Standard Stack already implied this with "matching gen"; the plan's transcription was the bug.
- **Fix:** Pinned `riverpod_generator: ^4.0.3` (the next 4.x major after annotation 4.0.2's matching pair).
- **Files modified:** `mobile/pubspec.yaml`
- **Verification:** `fvm flutter pub get` succeeds, `pubspec.lock` resolves 113 deps cleanly.
- **Committed in:** `323bdf5` (Task 2 commit)

**2. [Rule 1 — Bug] very_good_analysis lints failing on first analyze run**
- **Found during:** Task 3 (verification step)
- **Issue:** First `fvm flutter analyze` after writing main.dart + app.dart + smoke_test.dart reported 6 issues:

- **Fix:** Hoisted MaterialApp's `const` outward (Scaffold/Center/Text already const), wrapped the long comment, switched to package imports, sorted both pubspec dep blocks alphabetically, reordered smoke_test imports.
- **Files modified:** `mobile/lib/main.dart`, `mobile/lib/app.dart`, `mobile/pubspec.yaml`, `mobile/test/smoke_test.dart`
